About the event

Join us for an inspiring and dynamic event designed to bring passionate minds together.

This experience offers participants the opportunity to learn, collaborate, and challenge themselves in a motivating environment.

Whether you are looking to expand your network, gain new skills, or simply be part of an energetic community, this event is the perfect place to start.

Roles & participation

Participant

Engage in challenges, work solo or in a team, submit your work, and earn badges or recognition.

Staff

Support the event operations behind the scenes, ensure everything runs smoothly, and help participants as needed.

Mentor

Provide guidance, answer questions, and offer advice to help participants succeed in their tasks.

How it works?

Choose how you want to be involved in the event. Whether you want to compete as a participant, guide others as a mentor or support operations as staff, there’s a role for everyone. Each role comes with its own responsibilities and ways to contribute, ensuring everyone can make an impact.
